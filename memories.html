Memories

Relive your Photos from the Past

 

Memories: all the photos you took on this day in years past.

Features:

  • You remember the Everpix Flashback feature that everyone loved?
  • The one that Picturelife quickly implemented when Everpix was shutdown?
  • Well now Memories gives you that feature for iCloud Photo Library.
  • Memories shows you all your photos taken on the same date as today, in previous years.
  • It's a great way to relive old photos you might otherwise never look at again.
  • Get a notification each day to tell you how many memories you have that day.
  • Quickly see your memories in a lovely Today View widget.
  • Use the Date Picker or swipe up or down to see memories for a different date.
  • Share and favorite the photos you love, or Delete the ones you hate.

Universal:

  • Memories will work on all your iOS devices: iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch. Requires iOS 8 or higher.
